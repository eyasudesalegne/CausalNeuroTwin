# Code of Conduct

Project participants must communicate professionally, evaluate work on technical and scientific merit, respect confidentiality and data-use restrictions, and avoid harassment, discrimination, coercion, or retaliation.

Scientific disagreement is expected. Personal attacks, undisclosed conflicts of interest, misuse of participant data, manipulation of results, and unsupported clinical claims are unacceptable.

Concerns should be reported privately according to `SECURITY.md` or directly to the project maintainers. Reports will be handled proportionately, confidentially, and with attention to due process.
